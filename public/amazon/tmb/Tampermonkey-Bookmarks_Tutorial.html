TM Installation Steps

  1. Download both the TamperMonkey Addon & Scripts from the download buttons above.
      2. (Make sure to ALLOW THE POP-UP at the top of the screen FROM THIS PAGE when downloading the Scripts)

  2. Click the Tampermonkey icon then "⚙️Dashboard" from the top-right corner of your browser as in the video.
      3. If it's missing THEN click the puzzle-piece first!

  3. Then open the "Utilities" Menu and click "Browse" next to the "IMPORT from file" category.
  4. Select the downloaded Scripts file and hit ENTER or CLICK "import" to finish. 😁

any issues are user-error 🙄


OR

Bookmark Importing Steps

  1. Download the Bookmarks from the download button above.
      2. (Make sure to click DIRECTLY ON THE PINK text [your cursor should be the shape of a hand, not a pointer] on the download page to begin the download)

  2. Open your browser menu at the top-right corner and under "Bookmarks" click "Show bookmarks toolbar"
  3. Then re-open the Bookmarks menu and click "Manage bookmarks" at the bottom of the menu
  4. Select "IMPORT AND BACKUP" ➡️ "Restore" ➡️ "Choose File" and select the downloaded Bookmarks file 😁
